Lasagna

One of the best dishes ever!
Better tasted together.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bechamel
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Put a layer of bechamel in the tray
  2. Put a layer of tomato sauce over the bechamel
  3. Put a pasta sheet on the two layers
  4. Repeat until the tray is full
  5. Grate some parmesan on top
  6. Bake your lasagna 45 mins at 200Â°

Lovely tip

Serve it steaming hot!
